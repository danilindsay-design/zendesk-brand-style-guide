# Brand Voice & Copy Style

## Brand Philosophy

**"The customer is always human."**

When things go sideways, you want service you can count on. Zendesk ensures every interaction ends in resolution, helping brands show up for their customers exactly how they need to.

---

## Brand Attributes

### Approachable
**What it means:** Intuitive, welcoming, clear
**How it shows:** Use contractions, short sentences (10-20 words), conversational but professional tone

✅ "We'll help you get started"
❌ "We will assist in your onboarding process"

### Modern
**What it means:** Bold, forward-thinking, innovative
**How it shows:** Avoid dated business language, keep it fresh and current

✅ "Build better customer experiences"
❌ "Leverage synergies to enhance the customer journey paradigm"

### Trusted
**What it means:** Consistent, reliable, quality-focused
**How it shows:** Confident statements without hedging

✅ "We help teams resolve issues faster"
❌ "We might be able to help teams potentially resolve issues"

### Connected
**What it means:** Relevant, cultural, conversation-shaping
**How it shows:** Direct address using "you," "we," "us"

✅ "You can connect every channel"
❌ "Users can connect all available channels"

### Fresh
**What it means:** Clean, elegant, seamless
**How it shows:** Remove unnecessary words, one idea per sentence

✅ "AI that understands your customers"
❌ "Our advanced AI-powered solution leverages machine learning to understand and anticipate your customers' needs"

## Core Principles

### Active Voice, Not Passive

Makes our message clear, direct, and confident. Takes responsibility. Shows Zendesk is a reliable partner.

✅ **Active:** "Zendesk AI handles support requests efficiently"
❌ **Passive:** "Support requests are handled efficiently by Zendesk AI"

✅ **Active:** "We resolve issues faster"
❌ **Passive:** "Issues are resolved faster"

### Concise Copy

Respects audience's time, holds attention, increases likelihood of action.

- **Headline:** ~30 characters
- **Body copy:** ~80 characters per line, 2-3 sentences
- **Sentences:** 10-20 words average
- **Remove filler:** "very," "really," "just," "actually"

### Direct Address (You/We)

Fosters connection and inclusivity. Brings us closer to the reader, builds loyalty.

✅ "You can access features"
❌ "Users can access features"

✅ "We handle the complexity"
❌ "The platform handles the complexity"

### Strong, Confident Statements

Drives conversions, strengthens credibility. Clear messaging reinforces our authority as leader in AI-powered service.

✅ "Transform your service with AI"
❌ "AI might help improve your service"

✅ "Resolve issues faster"
❌ "Potentially resolve issues more quickly"

### Globally Inclusive Tone

Broadens reach, ensures message resonates across regions.

- Avoid region-specific idioms
- Use international date formats when needed
- Consider cultural sensitivities
- Localize spelling by region

## Copy Structure

### Supertitle / Eyebrow (Optional)

- Straightforward descriptor
- Good for SEO
- Best for section titles: "Overview," "Features," "Testimonials"
- Use Zendesk Mono, uppercase, 12px/700

**Examples:**
- "AI-Powered Customer Service"
- "Platform Overview"
- "Customer Success"

### Headline (~30 characters)

- Focus on one idea
- Lead with verb if possible
- Convey confidence and spark interest
- Use sentence case (only first letter capitalized)
- No period at end

**Examples:**
✅ "Transform your service with AI"
✅ "Resolve issues faster"
✅ "Connect every channel"

❌ "Transform Your Service With AI" (title case)
❌ "Transform your service with AI." (period)

### Body Copy (~80 characters per line)

- 2-3 sentences
- Speak to customer benefit or value
- Clear, concise, informative
- Use active voice
- Direct address (you/we)

**Example:**
"Zendesk AI helps you resolve issues faster and deliver experiences your customers love. We handle the complexity so you can focus on what matters—building relationships."

### Feature Headlines

- Make actionable
- Focus on what user can do
- Describe the value
- Remove jargon or confusing titles
- Use headline best practices

**Examples:**
✅ "Resolve issues faster"
✅ "Connect every channel"
✅ "Understand what matters"

❌ "Omnichannel Integration Capabilities"
❌ "Advanced Analytics Suite"

### CTAs (2-3 short active words)

- Lead with verb
- Keep consistent across pages for usability
- Follow standard conventions

**Examples:**
- "Start free trial"
- "Watch demo"
- "Contact Sales"
- "Get started"
- "Learn more"
- "Sign up free"

❌ "Click here"
❌ "Submit"
❌ "Learn more about our product"

## Technical Rules

### Capitalization

**Sentence case for headlines**
- Only first letter capitalized
- Exception: Proper nouns (Zendesk AI, CX Trends)

✅ "Get the best support for your team"
❌ "Get The Best Support For Your Team"

✅ "Zendesk AI transforms customer service"

### Punctuation

**No periods on headlines**
✅ "Transform your service"
❌ "Transform your service."

**No spaces around em dashes**
✅ "AI—powered by Zendesk"
❌ "AI — powered by Zendesk"

**No ampersands**
- Unless part of brand name

✅ "Sales and Marketing"
❌ "Sales & Marketing"

✅ "AT&T" (brand name exception)

**Oxford comma**
- Always use serial comma

✅ "Email, chat, and phone"
❌ "Email, chat and phone"

### Italics

Use italics ONLY for:
- News websites, magazines, newspapers
- Movies, plays, TV shows
- Podcasts, radio shows

✅ *The New York Times*
✅ *Interstellar*

❌ Emphasis or special terms (use bold instead)

### Trademarks

**Zendesk is an adjective, not a verb or noun**
- No article required
- Avoid possessives

✅ "Zendesk platform"
✅ "Zendesk employees"
✅ "Zendesk products"

❌ "Zendesk's platform"
❌ "The Zendesk"

**Don't add "Zen" to product names**

❌ "ZenSupport"
❌ "ZenChat"

✅ Use proper product names only

## AP Style (with exceptions)

Follow AP Style with these modifications:

✅ **Always use Oxford comma**
- "Email, chat, and phone"

✅ **Numerals okay as design element**
- "3 ways to improve service"
- "50% faster resolution"

✅ **No spaces around em dashes**
- "AI—powered by Zendesk"

✅ **No ampersands** (unless part of brand name)

## Language Rules

### Avoid Jargon and Business Clichés

❌ **Never use:**
- "Leverage" (use "use" or "apply")
- "Synergy" (use "collaboration" or "teamwork")
- "Paradigm shift" (use "transformation" or "change")
- "Circle back" (use "follow up" or "revisit")
- "Move the needle" (use "make progress" or "improve")
- "Low-hanging fruit" (use "easy wins" or "quick wins")
- "Think outside the box" (use "be creative" or "innovate")
- "Best-in-class" (use "leading" or "top-rated")
- "World-class" (use specific benefits instead)
- "Solutions" (use specific product/feature names)

✅ **Use instead:**
- Clear, specific language
- Active verbs
- Concrete benefits
- Simple words

### Avoid Acronyms

- Unless more common than full term
- Spell out on first use

✅ "Artificial Intelligence (AI)"
✅ "CEO" (more common than Chief Executive Officer)

❌ "CX" without context
❌ "KPI" without explanation

### Avoid Unnecessary Gender References

Use gender-neutral language:

✅ "They," "them," "their" (singular)
✅ "The customer," "the user"
✅ "Team members," "sales reps"

❌ "He or she"
❌ "His or her"

## Localization

### Spelling

Localize spelling based on region:

**US:**
- "optimize"
- "analyze"  
- "color"
- "center"

**UK/AU/NZ:**
- "optimise"
- "analyse"
- "colour"
- "centre"

### Be Consistent

- Choose one variant per market
- Use consistently throughout all copy
- Update localization guide for translators

## Voice Examples

### On-Brand ✅

"Transform your service with AI that understands"
- Active voice ("transform")
- Direct address ("your")
- Concise
- Leads with verb
- Confident, not hedging

"Zendesk AI helps you resolve issues faster. We handle the complexity so you can focus on customers."
- Active voice throughout
- Direct address (you, we)
- Short sentences
- Clear benefit
- No jargon

"Start free trial"
- Active verb first
- Concise (3 words)
- Clear action
- No unnecessary words

### Off-Brand ❌

"Service transformation solutions may potentially be leveraged to enhance customer experience paradigms"
- Passive construction
- Jargon ("leverage," "paradigms," "solutions")
- Hedging ("may potentially")
- No direct address
- Too long and unclear

"Issues can be resolved by utilizing our platform's advanced capabilities"
- Passive voice
- No direct address
- Business jargon ("utilizing," "capabilities")
- Unclear benefit

"Click here to learn more about our comprehensive suite of solutions"
- Vague CTA ("click here")
- Jargon ("comprehensive suite of solutions")
- Too long
- Not action-oriented

## Copy Checklist

Before publishing any copy, verify:

- [ ] Active voice (not passive)
- [ ] Direct address (you/we, not "users" or "customers")
- [ ] Sentence case headlines
- [ ] No periods on headlines
- [ ] No jargon or business clichés
- [ ] Concise (removed unnecessary words)
- [ ] Strong, confident (not hedging)
- [ ] Oxford commas
- [ ] No spaces around em dashes
- [ ] No ampersands (unless brand name)
- [ ] Proper Zendesk usage (adjective, not possessive)
- [ ] Accessible (don't rely on color/visual alone)
- [ ] Globally inclusive
- [ ] Spell-checked for target region

## Quick Reference

**Do:**
- Use active voice
- Use "you" and "we"
- Keep it concise
- Lead with verbs
- Be confident
- Use sentence case
- Use Oxford comma

**Don't:**
- Use passive voice
- Use jargon
- Use possessive with Zendesk
- Use title case
- Add periods to headlines
- Use ampersands
- Add spaces around em dashes
- Rely on color alone for meaning
